Jiraiya

The Man Who Is An Exceptionally Powerful Shinobi

The Abilities Of Jiraiya Sensei

  • Chakra and Physical Prowess - Jiraiya had chakra reserves large enough to summon Gamabunta and chakra potent enough to use senjutsu.
  • Ninjutsu - Jiraiya was a ninjutsu master,[36] famed as the "Toad Sage" (蝦蟇仙人, Gama Sennin) because of his affiliation with the toads of Mount Myōboku.
  • Senjutsu - Jiraiya was taught how to use senjutsu by Fukasaku, which he can use to increase his speed, durability, and physical strength.[40] He can also use it to empower his other ninjutsu, such as the Ultra-Big Ball Rasengan.
  • Intelligence - Despite his goofy and eccentric nature, Jiraiya was actually very shrewd in the field. His specialty is intelligence gathering, having a spy network that runs throughout the Five Great Shinobi Countries that he can use to gain information about a particular person or group to be used to his or his allies' advantage.
